Financial Formulas

Return on Equity (ROE)

$$ROE = \frac{Net\ Income}{Shareholder's\ Equity} \times 100$$

Our Q4 ROE: 24.8%

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

$$CAGR = \left(\frac{Ending\ Value}{Beginning\ Value}\right)^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1$$

3-Year Revenue CAGR: 18.2%

These are the key formulas we use for performance evaluation. ROE of 24.8% exceeds industry average of 15-20%. Our CAGR demonstrates consistent, sustainable growth.
